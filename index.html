About

Some engineers know how to build, others just think they do, but one thing they all have in common is that they need to take a shower.

In the spirit of unserious ideas, Shower Hacks has only one judging criteria: Shock Value.

You can build anything... anything, like an AI that automatically determines health insurance premiums based on attractiveness as a health indicator. A social media platform that only accepts users with an 800+ credit score. An AI situationship that texts you but then ghosts you randomly. A dating app that matches based on genetic compatibility. A map app that gives the location of people in the area who need a hug. Dating app for infidelity. An AI jury that makes decisions on criminal cases. An AI girlfriend that sends the user CashApp requests. An Uber alternative where you get to select the personal demographics of your driver. A backlink spam bot to hurt a competitor's SEO. AI-powered deep search to perform a background check on your bf or gf. A subscription-based AI homework cheating SAAS that accepts EBT. A C2C platform to trade prescriptions. Endless opportunities...
